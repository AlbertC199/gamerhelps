What genre of games help with mental health?

Gaming Genres to Look Into


There are a variety of gaming genres to choose from that can help in certain skills or even help you calm yourself after a stressful day. Video Game genres like simulation, tycoon, racing, MMOs, and etc. The main video game genres I'll be talking about are:


1. FPS (Shooters)


The skills FPS (Shooters) games can improve are; improved focus, reaction time, problem-solving, quick thinking, and even hand-eye coordination. FPS games can be frustrating at first but once you get the hang of it you'll be having a good time playing them. FPS makes you think out of side of the box on ways to get the upperhand on your enemy. Either, it's playing objective or wiping out the enemy team. There are many ways you can win in an online match. In Call of Duty (COD), you can decide to play the story mode and get immersed into the story-driven action adventure that the campaign has to offer. Or you can play the higly competitive multiplayer mode and go against players like you. Who want to reach the top of the leaderboards and be the very best in the COD community.


2. Adventure


When you want something that catches your attention and when it comes to story-driven games. Adventure games is the move and is filled with a lot content to keep you occupied. Keeping you busy with it's vast open-world and side activites. Open-world adventure games are also a good way to keep yourself distracted and entertained. Adventure games can also encourage active participation and motivate players to go outside and see what the real world is like. The Witcher 3 offer countless amount of missions and side missions. Even some people we call NPCs will have bounties where you have to kill something or someone. Amongst the gameplay, is the majestic vast open-world environment The Witcher 3 has to offer.


3. Fighting


Fighting games can help with critical thinking and rapid decision making. They are also very exciting to watch and learn from. With games like UFC, you can actually learn certain moves and even learn how to throw different type of punches that work in certain scenarios to throw off your opponents. Playing some physical games in VR can help you improve your movement skills. In the game Beat Saber, you have to strike the beats of the song you end up choosing and you have to physically move around. You also have to time your hits by swinging the contoller upward or downward.


4. Sandbox


Sandbox games help with imagination and creativity. It's a type of genre that give the player freedom to their own imagination. Helps them relax and shape the game into their type of world. In games like Minecraft, you can build almost anything you want with blocks and even destroy if you would like to. Minecraft is one of the top sandbox games out and many people love it for the building aspect of it.


5. Puzzle


There are a huge variety of puzzle games that are out right now and eve some being made today. Puzzle games can help keep your mind occupied and reduce stress because they distract our brain with finding patterns, which then triggers a hormone response and lowers cortisol. The different kind of puzzle games you can give a try that help are; match-three puzzle, action puzzle, word games, jigsaw puzzles, and trivia games.
